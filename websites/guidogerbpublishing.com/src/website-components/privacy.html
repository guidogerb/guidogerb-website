Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our utmost priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we protect your data and your rights when using our platform. We believe in transparency and respect for your personal information.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or other identifying information.
  • Geolocation data or any other device-related information.
  • Browsing history, app usage statistics, or playback preferences.

Our platform is designed to allow you to enjoy music without compromising your privacy. You can use our services without signing up, logging in, or providing any personal information.

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking tools to monitor your activity. Unlike many platforms, we intentionally avoid integrating tracking scripts or third-party analytics software. This ensures:

  • No monitoring of your listening habits or content preferences.
  • No tracking of your IP address, device type, or location.
  • No sharing of anonymized or aggregated data with any third parties.

No Third-Party Integrations

Our platform does not rely on third-party services or integrations that might compromise your privacy. All interactions occur securely within our system, without involving any external parties.

Commitment to Privacy

We are committed to maintaining a completely private and secure user experience. If our policies or services change, we will ensure that users are informed and have the ability to make informed decisions about their privacy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us at support@guidogerbpublishing.com. We are happy to provide further information or clarification.
